Winter's coming on! All those other squirrels are trying to hoard the acorns for themselves. Collect them, quarrel for them, even ambush your friends before you're left with nothing to survive on. In the end, the quickest, most plump squirrel wins! Do you have what it takes to survive as a squirrel?
